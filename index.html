What is stance nation about...?

What is Stance:Nation all about? Simply put, we are here to promote the “aggressive fitment” movement and showcase some of the latest and greatest cars that are pushing the aggressive fitment limits in one way or another.

Stance is a style of car customization derived from auto sports such as Formula 1 and drifting. Typically high power output cars designed for the race tracks feature extremely low and stiff suspension along with light and wide sport wheels for better handling and cornering on the race tracks
